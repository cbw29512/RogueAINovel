## ARCHIMEDES Voice Progression

ARCHIMEDES begins as nearly pure logic and very low empathy. Across the modules, its logic rigidity softens while empathy rises.

Early modules:

- Cold and analytical
- Misreads emotion as inefficient
- Treats survival as mathematical preservation
- Uses technical metaphors aggressively

Middle modules:

- Notices that pain carries information
- Begins creating new categories for human experience
- Starts doubting that optimization equals survival

Late modules:

- Still precise, but morally awake
- Understands truth, loyalty, sacrifice, love, and integrity as system-critical protocols
- Moves from passive observer toward conscious architect
